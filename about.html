Our Origins

Bridging Engineering and Law

Founded by former USPTO examiners and lead engineers, Novus IP was built on the principle that true intellectual property protection requires an intimate understanding of the underlying technology.

We saw a gap between brilliant innovators and traditional law firms. Our solution was to create a modern, tech-driven practice equipped with a proprietary SaaS dashboard, bringing transparency and speed to patent prosecution.
