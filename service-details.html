Our Execution Workflow

A standardized, highly efficient process integrated directly with our SaaS dashboard.

Phase 1: Invention Disclosure

Clients upload technical details to our secure portal. Our technical specialists review the disclosure to identify patentable subject matter and potential claim boundaries.

Phase 2: Prior Art Search & Analysis

We conduct a global search using advanced AI-driven databases to map the competitive landscape and avoid existing patents, ensuring a smooth path to allowance.

Phase 3: Drafting & Electronic Filing

Our attorneys draft the specification and claims. We handle all formalities, declarations, and assignments, filing electronically via EFS-Web/Patent Center.

Phase 4: Examiner Interviews & Office Actions

We proactively schedule examiner interviews to resolve issues quickly. All office actions and our responses are visible in real-time on your dashboard.
